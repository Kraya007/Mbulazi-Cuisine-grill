About Us

Indyebo Foundation – Indyebo Foundation is a non-profit, community-based organization committed to fighting hunger and reducing food waste. By collecting surplus food from households, restaurants, supermarkets, and farms, the NGO redistributes it to underprivileged individuals and families in need.

Driven by a vision of a hunger-free society, Indyebo Foundation works with volunteers, donors, and partners to ensure that no good food goes to waste and that no person goes to bed hungry. The organization provides immediate relief while also raising awareness about food insecurity, poverty, and sustainable development.
